About Me

My Story

I love learning and gathering data, extracting little nuggets of insight, and transforming them into tangible solutions knowing that the work I am doing can make someone's day a little bit better. Before entering the world of UX, I was a middle school teacher for over 10 years - I loved that I could support others, help them solve practical problems, and see the world from many perspectives as I got to interact with so many people who had a wide range of experiences to share.

I found that the things I loved as an educator were also present in the world of UX, and not one to shy away from a challenge and adventure, I now apply my skills and experience as a UX researcher and designer in Seattle, WA. In my free time, you will often find me upside down practicing acrobatics and handstands. Right side up, I am a competitive gamer, rock climber, yoga practitioner, and cultural and culinary explorer.
